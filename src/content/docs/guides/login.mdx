### Steps to Login
Login means that you are an existing AHQ Store user, i.e. you have an EMail ID and Password
- Enter your EMail ID
- Enter your Password
- Click login

### SignUp
To create a new account on AHQ Store do to following
- Click "Create your account!"
- Enter your EMail ID 
- Click Continue
- Enter the Password you wish to keep
- Click Continue

:::tip[Note]
If SignUp is successful, you'll be redirected to the "Login" screen, simply enter your new credentials to login
:::
